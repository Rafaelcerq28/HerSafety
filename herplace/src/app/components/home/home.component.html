A place to find the perfect place

HerPlace is a web application designed to women by providing them real-time safety insights and comfort evaluations for bars and clubs. In many cities, women face concerns about safety and comfort in nightlife, HerPlace is a crowd-sourced platform where users can access detailed safety information, such as reports of harassment, uncomfortable situations, or unsafe environments, it also rates places on how well they accommodate womenâ€™s needs, from the availability of feminine hygiene products to the general comfort of the atmosphere.


Through a search interface, women can explore places, view safety ratings, and read personalized tips, the application uses AI to generate custom safety recommendations based on user preferences and location, creating a personalised experience. Integrating a rating system for comfort and safety, HerPlace encourages nightlife venues to improve their services and adapt to a more inclusive environment for women.


In addition to its safety focus, HerPlace promotes community engagement, allowing users to report incidents, share feedback on the environment, and support others in making informed choices, with HerPlace women can enjoy their nights out with confidence, knowing they have access to critical information that prioritizes their well-being.
